Sea Life Park is Hawaii's best
wild life and wild animal park

Offering fun and educational animal encounters for the entire family. With a focus on education and conservation, the shows, exhibits and interactive programs offer thrilling, once-in-a-lifetime up-close experiences with Hawaii's marine life. Each Luau ticket includes General Admission to Sea Life Park, to redeem same-day or within one (1) year after Luau date.


Get inspired by amazing marine animals during a show or by observing them in their habitats with your general admission ticket. If you're looking for a more unique experience, you can swim with dolphins, sharks, sea lions or Hawaiian rays with an animal interactive program upgrade!
