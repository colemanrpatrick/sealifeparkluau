frequently asked questions

  • Hours of Operation

    Sunday | Monday | Tuesday | Wednesday | Thursday | Friday

    Sea Life Park Hawaii
    Daily, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

  • What happens if it rains?

    We know many people have limited time here in Hawaii to experience a Luau. For this reason, the Luau runs in rain or shine. Our reservations department will contact you if there is a cancellation due to weather. See cancellation policy.

  • Can I use my admission to Sea Life Park before my luau?

    You can use your included admission to Sea Life Park the same-day as your Luau, or within 1 year from your Luau date. Your admission tickets to Sea Life Park will be included with your Luau confirmation email. Admission is non-transferrable and must match the name on your reservation.Click Here for more info.

  • Can I sit with someone in a separate reservation?

    You can enter the last names of any parties or separate reservations that you are joining at the Luau if you would like to be seated together. If you have already made your reservation and need to add this request, please email us at info@alohakailuau.com or send us a live chat message here on the site!

  • Disabilities or Special Accommodations

    Our goal is to make all of our guests comfortable! If you require special accommodations, have a wheelchair or have any other special requests - please contact us at (808) 926-3800 at least 48 hours in advance of your Luau date. Sea Life Park and the Luau grounds are ADA accessible.

  • How do I book with my Go City Pass?

    Included as a Premium Attraction. Premiums only included on 3+ Day All-Inclusive Passes. To make a reservation using your Go City All-Inclusive Pass, click here or give our reservations department a call at (808) 926-3800 with those card numbers handy and someone from our team will be happy to assist you.

  • Do you offer large group discounts?

    We love helping you celebrate your special occasions, birthdays or life events! If you have a large group (30 people or more), please email us at info@alohakailuau.com

  • Cancellation Policy

    Cancellations must go through Ka Moana Luau. No cash surrender unless at least twenty-four (24) hours advance notice of cancellation is given. No-Shows and cancellations received within a twenty-four (24) hour period are subject to the entire activity cost and processing fees. No same day cancellations will be accepted. Cancellations on behalf of Ka Moana Luau will result in full refunds.

  • My Question is not on this list

    We're here to help make your experience in Hawaii, whether you're a local or a visitor, unforgettable! If you have an urgent question, questions about making a booking or a pre-made reservation, please call or email our reservations department at (808) 926-3800 or reservations@alohakailuau.com. Other inquiries please email info@alohakailuau.com
