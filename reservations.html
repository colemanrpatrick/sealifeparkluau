    We offer comfortable round-trip transportation from several convenient pick-up locations in Waikiki and surrounding areas for just $25/person.

    If you book your luau tickets with added transportation, your exact pick-up time and location will be in your confirmation email. For same day reservations, the cut-off time to book your luau with roundtrip transportation is 12:00 pm.

    There is an option for early hotel pick-up for guests who wish to redeem their included admission to Sea Life Park the same day as their luau. To book, select the "EARLY Pick-up" option when making your reservation. Early transportation to the luau needs to be requested by 10:00 am the day of your luau reservation.

    Select your transportation options during checkout. Additionally, self-parking is available at Sea Life ark for $15/car.

    ADA accessible vehicles are available with 48-hour advance notice. Please email at sales@hawaiidiscount.com to request.
